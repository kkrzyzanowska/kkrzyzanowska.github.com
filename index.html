  • About

    I am a PhD student at the Faculty of Philosophy, University of Groningen where I work under the supervision of Igor Douven. My research interests include semantics and pragmatics of conditionals, evidentiality in language, and psychology of reasoning.

    I studied philosophy at the University of Warsaw and, as an exchange student, at the Katholieke Universiteit Leuven. I obtained my master's degree in philosophy in September 2009. My thesis on belief reports in the perspective of philosophy of language and cognitive science was supervised by Justyna Grudzińska. Since November 2009, I am a member of the Formal Epistemology Project.
