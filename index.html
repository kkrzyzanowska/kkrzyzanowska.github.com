  • Home

    I am a PhD student at the Department of Theoretical Philosophy, Faculty of Philosophy, University of Groningen where I work under the supervision of Igor Douven.
    I received my Master's degree in the Institute of Philosophy at the University of Warsaw in 2009. My master's thesis on belief reports from linguistic and cognitive perspective was supervised by Justyna Grudzińska. I am affiliated with the Formal Epistemology Project.
