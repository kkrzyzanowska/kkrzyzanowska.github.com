  • About

    I am a postdoctoral research fellow at the Munich Center for Mathematical Philosophy, Ludwig-Maximilians-Universität München. Before coming to Munich, I was a PhD student at the Faculty of Philosophy, University of Groningen where I worked under the supervision of Igor Douven.

    On 2 February 2015, I defended my doctoral thesis titled Between "If" and "Then:" Towards an Empirically Informed Philosphy of Conditionals. Here you can find more information about the defence.

    My research interests include semantics and pragmatics of conditionals, their role in reasoning and decision making, various issues related to semantics-pragmatics interface, and cognitive science.
